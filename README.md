# Customer Churn Analysis

An end-to-end Customer Churn Analysis project built using **Python, SQL (SQLite), Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, and Seaborn**. The project analyzes customer subscription behavior to identify churn patterns, customer risk segments, revenue loss, Customer Lifetime Value (CLTV) loss, and actionable business insights.

---

## Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges for subscription-based businesses. This project combines SQL and Python-based analytics to:

- Clean and preprocess customer data
- Merge multiple relational datasets
- Perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Analyze churn behavior
- Calculate business metrics
- Generate actionable business recommendations

## Business Insights

- Overall Churn Rate: **36.63%**
- Customer Retention Rate: **63.37%**
- Average Revenue Per User (ARPU): **₹16.82**
- Average Customer Tenure: **1,285 Days**
- Premium plan customers exhibit the highest churn rate.
- Karnataka records the highest customer churn.
- Total Revenue at Risk due to churn: **₹606.62**
- Estimated Customer Lifetime Value (CLTV) Loss: **₹9,748**
- Customers with higher complaint counts and escalations are significantly more likely to churn.
- High-risk customers contribute the majority of expected revenue loss.

---

## Recommendations

- Prioritize retention campaigns for Premium plan customers.
- Encourage Monthly subscribers to switch to Annual plans through discounts and loyalty benefits.
- Improve customer support response time to reduce complaint-driven churn.
- Investigate the reasons behind high churn in Karnataka.
- Monitor high-risk customers proactively using churn prediction scores.
- Reduce customer complaints through faster issue resolution and improved service quality.
